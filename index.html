MY TEAM

Mr. Sunshine

Mr. Sunshine follows a young boy born into slavery who, after a life-altering event, escapes to the United States during the 1871 Shinmiyangyo. Years later, he returns to Joseon as a United States Marine Corps officer, carrying the weight of his past. There, he meets and falls in love with a nobleman's daughter, whose strong will and secret ties to the resistance make their love both fateful and bittersweet.

뿅
Twenty Five Twenty One

In a time when dreams feel out of reach, an ambitious teen fencer refuses to give up on her passion, determined to carve her own path. Along the way, she meets a hardworking young man struggling to rebuild his life after losing everything. As they navigate love, friendship, and the pursuit of their dreams, their journey unfolds in a story filled with warmth, resilience, and bittersweet moments.

뿅
Revenant

The series follows a young woman who becomes possessed by a mysterious entity, drawing her into a world of darkness she never imagined. A professor of folklore, gifted with the ability to see ghosts, takes an interest in her case. As they cross paths, they uncover unsettling secrets and seek the truth behind a string of eerie suicides haunting the city, drawing them deeper into a chilling web of supernatural events.

뿅
Jeongnyeon: The Star is Born

"Jeongnyeon: The Star Is Born" is a South Korean television series set in the 1950s, after the Korean War. It follows the journey of Yoon Jeongnyeon, a young girl from Mokpo with a natural talent for singing but no money or formal education. sets out on a path filled with challenges, ambition, and the pursuit of stardom in a rapidly changing world.
